RHS Racing at the Howard of Effingham School

RHS Racing consists of a team of year 8 students at the Howard of Effingham School. We have a diverse range of interests in design, engineering, science, and maths which we are channelling into our project, as well as applying new skills in business and finance.

F1 in Schools

F1 in Schools is an international STEM competition for secondary school students, in which small groups students design and manufacture a miniature F1 car using CAD/CAM and CAE design tools, as well as learning the business side of running a Formula 1 team.
